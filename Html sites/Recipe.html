Simple Poutine Recipe!

In the case that any of you would like to make this wonderful Canadian dish here is a simple recipe. Keep in mind you could switch some of these ingredients out for other things or add some new ones to make you dish more unique! with that let’s move onto the actual recipe now!

For this recipe you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 kg of potatoes
  • 450g of gravy (your choice what kind)
  • 400g of cheese curds (your choice what kind)
  • Sea Salt (or table salt if unavalible)
  • Oil(preferably peanut or any vegetable oil)

After you have all these ingredients you can start making this dish!

  1. Cut the potatoes (skin on or off) into french fries and then soak it in water inside the refrigerator for 4 hours, to drawing out the starch
  2. Meanwhile heat the oil to about 200°C
  3. After heating the oil remove the potatoes from the water and dry them with a kitchen towel
  4. Fry the potatoes till they become a wonderful golden colour
  5. Meanwhile you can heat the gravy or do it after you are done the fries
  6. After you remove the fries from the oil, sprinkle it with sea salt
  7. Mix the fries with cheese curds and coat with gravy

Serve warm and then enjoy!

(Canadian Encyclopedia, 2019)
